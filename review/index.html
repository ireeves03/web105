Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an open world anime rpg, in which the player completes quests for rewards and the push the story forward. The game is consistently updated, adding more content with each update.

Character Roster

Genshin Impact runs off of a "Gacha" system, essentially like gambling. There is an extensive character list, each charatcer has unique designs and stories a well as unqiue combat gameplay. In order for the player to obtain characters, they have to roll for them on certain rate-up banners. Although the game is free-to-play and players can get enough rewards to save up for certain characters, there are many micro-transactions for the in game currency that is quite tempting.

Combat

The combat system is free turn and relys on strategy and an understanding of the elemental reactions in-game. There are seven elements, anemo (wind), hydro (water), geo (earth), pyro (fire), electro (electricity), cryo (ice), and dendro (plant). Which all combine in different ways for different buffs and reactions, allowing for diverse gameplay and a numerous amount of strategies for clearing content. The game allows for multi-player in the open-world, mostly for combat in domains and boss fights.
